# Support

Need help with Waffle, hit a bug, or have a question? We'd love to hear from you.

Email us at **[wafflingwafflerswaffle@gmail.com](mailto:wafflingwafflerswaffle@gmail.com)** and we'll get back to you as soon as we can.

## What to include

To help us help you faster, it's useful to mention:

- The device and OS version you're using (e.g. iPhone 15, iOS 18).
- What you were trying to do, and what happened instead.
- A screenshot or screen recording, if it's relevant.

## Account & data requests

To delete your account, or to request removal of specific photos, audio, or video, email the address above and we'll take care of it.
